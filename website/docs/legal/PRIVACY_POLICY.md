# LazaiTrader Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

---

## INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how LazaiTrader ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our automated cryptocurrency trading platform (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a functional and secure trading experience. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

---

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you use LazaiTrader, we collect:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Telegram User ID** | Account identification and bot interaction | Database |
| **Telegram Username** | Display and communication | Database |
| **Wallet Address (EOA)** | Wallet ownership verification, withdrawals | Database |
| **Smart Contract Wallet Address** | Trading operations | Database |
| **Trading Configuration** | Execute your trading strategy | Database |

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you interact with the Service, we automatically collect:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Command History** | Service functionality, debugging | Temporary logs |
| **Trade Execution Records** | Trading history, auditing | Database |
| **Timestamps** | Service operation, trade timing | Database |
| **Error Logs** | Debugging, service improvement | Temporary logs |

### 1.3 Blockchain Data

By nature of blockchain technology, certain data is publicly recorded:

- Your Smart Contract Wallet address
- All transactions executed by your wallet
- Token balances and transfers
- Interaction with DEX smart contracts

**This blockchain data is public, permanent, and outside our control.**

### 1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect or have access to:

- Your private keys or seed phrases
- Personal identity documents
- Email addresses or phone numbers
- IP addresses or precise location data
- Financial information beyond wallet addresses
- Biometric data

## 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

### 2.1 Primary Uses

We use collected information to:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Operate the trading bot and execute your configured strategies | Contract performance |
| Deploy and manage your Smart Contract Wallet | Contract performance |
| Process withdrawals to your registered wallet | Contract performance |
| Send you trade notifications and alerts | Contract performance |
| Respond to your support requests | Legitimate interest |

### 2.2 Secondary Uses

We may also use information to:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Debug issues and improve the Service | Legitimate interest |
| Detect and prevent fraud or abuse | Legitimate interest |
| Comply with legal obligations | Legal requirement |
| Analyze aggregate usage patterns (anonymized) | Legitimate interest |

### 2.3 What We Do NOT Do

We do NOT:

- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing without consent
- Create detailed profiles for purposes beyond service operation
- Track you across other platforms or services

---

## 3. DATA SHARING

### 3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share data with service providers who assist in operating the Service:

| Provider Type | Data Shared | Purpose |
|---------------|-------------|---------|
| **Cloud Infrastructure** (Cloudflare) | Encrypted service data | Hosting and execution |
| **Database Provider** | User and trade data | Data storage |
| **Blockchain RPC Providers** | Transaction data | Blockchain interaction |

All service providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

### 3.2 Blockchain Networks

When executing trades, transaction data is broadcast to:

- The Metis Andromeda blockchain network
- Decentralized exchange smart contracts
- Public blockchain explorers

**This data becomes permanently public on the blockchain.**

### 3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by:

- Court order or legal process
- Law enforcement requests
- Regulatory requirements
- Protection of our legal rights

### 3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user data may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

---

## 4. DATA RETENTION

### 4.1 Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| Account information | Duration of account + 1 year | Service operation, disputes |
| Trade history | Duration of account + 3 years | Auditing, legal compliance |
| Configuration data | Duration of account | Service operation |
| Error logs | 30 days | Debugging |
| Command logs | 7 days | Service operation |

### 4.2 Blockchain Data

Transaction data recorded on the blockchain is **permanent and cannot be deleted**. This includes:

- All executed trades
- Wallet deployments
- Token transfers

### 4.3 Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account data:

1. Withdraw all funds from your Smart Contract Wallet
2. Contact us through official channels to request deletion
3. We will delete off-chain data within 30 days

Note: On-chain data cannot be deleted.

## 5. DATA SECURITY

### 5.1 Security Measures

We implement security measures including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security reviews
- Secure infrastructure (Cloudflare Workers)

### 5.2 Non-Custodial Security

Because LazaiTrader is non-custodial:

- We never have access to your private keys
- We cannot move funds without your authorization
- Smart contract architecture limits potential damage from any breach

### 5.3 Security Limitations

Despite our efforts:

- No system is 100% secure
- Beta software may have undiscovered vulnerabilities
- Smart contracts are unaudited
- Third-party services may have their own security issues

### 5.4 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

- Keeping your Telegram account secure
- Protecting your wallet private keys
- Using secure devices and networks
- Reporting suspected security issues
