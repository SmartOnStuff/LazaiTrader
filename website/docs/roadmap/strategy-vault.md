## FAQs

### How is performance verified?

All trades execute on-chain through your SCW. Performance is calculated from verified blockchain transactions, not self-reported data.

### Can creators cheat?

No. Only actual executed trades count. You can't publish fake history.

### What if a strategy stops working?

You can stop copying anytime. Historical performance doesn't guarantee future results - always manage risk appropriately.

### Is my identity revealed?

Only if you choose. Creators can remain anonymous while still having verified track records.
